About

Ingrid Helland

(b. 2000) is a visual artist and illustrator based in Oslo. She is educated at the Oslo National Academy of the Arts (KHiO) and the Bergen Academy of Art (KiB). Helland works primarily with drawing, most often focusing on figurative motifs built up through time-consuming details, symbols, allusions, and emotions. Her works may contain eerie, gothic, absurd, or fascinating imagery, or take the form of visual narratives drawn from the artist’s own life.

She is currently an intern with visual artist Sverre Malling.
